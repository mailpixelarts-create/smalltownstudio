2024 — Feature Film

The Last Frame

2h 14m Runtime
R Rating
2.39:1 Aspect Ratio

In the twilight of his career, legendary cinematographer Marcus Reid faces a choice that will define his legacy. When a young filmmaker offers him one final project — a deeply personal story about memory and loss — Marcus must confront the ghosts of his past while crafting what may be his last frame.

A meditation on art, mortality, and the stories we leave behind, "The Last Frame" is a visually stunning exploration of what it means to create in the face of endings.
