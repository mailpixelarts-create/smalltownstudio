About

Small Town Studio

An initiative by Aritra and Ayushman Gupta, STS is steeped in lived-in experience of the various nooks and corners of India. Relying on their childhood in Varanasi, one of the oldest living cities in the world, and education in Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore and Delhi, Small Town Studio aims to marry stories from urban and B town India and present relatable content that has both emotions and insights.

Combiningly, we have over 17+ years of experience in writing, directing and producing ad films and videos for various renowned brands as well as TV channels. Aritra specialises in sports, having written and directed for organisations like Sony, Zee, Neo and Disney. Ayushman's forte is humour, having created the once popular YT channel 'Being Indian' and numerous viral videos.

As creators our expertise is in understanding cultural insights, the zeitgeist and the emotional graph of the story and the characters. Our aim is to marry beautiful visuals with creative storytelling that prioritises the brand narrative but is also aesthetically pleasing.
